Final Year Project
Our Mission

Revolutionizing Soybean Farming with AI

This Final Year Project leverages deep Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and modern machine learning techniques to analyze soybean plant imagery. The system is designed to assist farmers, researchers and agronomists in making data-driven decisions to improve crop management and reduce yield loss.

Our platform provides real-time disease detection, variety identification, growth stage prediction and personalized AI recommendations — all from a single uploaded photograph.
