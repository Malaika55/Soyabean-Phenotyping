Tips for Best Results

1
Clear Lighting

Use natural daylight. Avoid harsh shadows or direct sunlight glare.

2
Full Plant View

Capture the entire plant from base to top. Include soil line if possible.

3
Sharp Focus

Hold steady, use focus mode. Blurry images reduce accuracy.

4
Simple Background

Avoid cluttered backgrounds. Plain soil or sky works best.
